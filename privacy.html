PagePilot AI — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026. Applies to PagePilot AI version 1.0.0.

PagePilot AI ("the extension") is a Chrome extension that summarizes the webpage you are viewing and lets you chat with and save those summaries. It is designed so that the developer learns nothing about you.

What the extension stores on your device

Using chrome.storage.local, the extension stores: your chosen AI provider (Groq or OpenAI); your API key; your settings (auto mode, summary language); your license record; a daily usage counter (date, count, last summarized URL); and your saved-summary library (URLs, titles, summaries, key points). This data never leaves your device. You can delete the library at any time from Settings, and removing the extension deletes all of it.

What leaves your device, and when

Only two kinds of network requests are ever made, both initiated by your actions:

  1. Summaries and chat → your chosen provider. When a summary or chat answer is generated (by you, or by auto mode when you open an article), the extracted text of the page you are viewing is sent directly from your browser to api.groq.com or api.openai.com, authenticated with your own API key. The provider's processing of that request is governed by their own terms and privacy policy. The developer never sees, proxies, or stores this data.
  2. License activation → Gumroad. When you click "Activate" in Settings, your license key and the product id pagepilot-ai are sent to api.gumroad.com to verify your Pro purchase. Nothing else is sent, and this only happens at activation.

What the extension does NOT do

  • No accounts, sign-ups, or identifiers.
  • No telemetry, analytics, crash reporting, or tracking of any kind.
  • No browsing-history access: the extension only ever reads the single page you currently have open, at the moment a summary is requested.
  • No sale, rental, or transfer of any user data to any third party for any purpose unrelated to the extension's core function.

Permissions

The extension's permissions (sidePanel, storage, activeTab, tabs, scripting, contextMenus, and host access to all URLs, api.groq.com, api.openai.com, and api.gumroad.com) are each required for the single purpose described above and are used for nothing else.

Children

The extension does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13, because it collects no information at all.

Changes

Any change to this policy will ship with a new extension version and be visible in the repository's CHANGELOG.

Contact

For questions about this policy, contact the developer through the Chrome Web Store support link or the repository listed on the extension's store page.
